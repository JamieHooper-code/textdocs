ART IDEAS
=========


3D PRINTED LINOCUT BLOCKS
-------------------------
Using 3D prints as the relief block instead of carving linoleum. Print the
design, ink it, press it like a normal lino block. Lets you do fine detail,
repeat-identical blocks, and shapes that would be painful to carve by hand.
