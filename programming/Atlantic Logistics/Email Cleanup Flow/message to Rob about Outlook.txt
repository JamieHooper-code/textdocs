========================


== SWEEP ==

Sweep is a built-in Outlook feature that automates handling of emails from a
specific sender. No technical setup required — just right-click any email,
hover Sweep, and pick an option.

Options:
- Keep only the most recent email from this sender
- Delete all emails from this sender (past and future)
- Move all emails from this sender to a folder

Good for: newsletters, automated notifications, vendor emails you want out of
the inbox. Can knock out a lot of inbox clutter in minutes with no IT involvement.

Limitation: sender-only — no filtering by subject or keywords, and no
time-based rules (e.g. "delete after 7 days" is not possible with Sweep).
