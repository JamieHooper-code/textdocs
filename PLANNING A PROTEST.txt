NECESSARY ELEMENTS:
	Key Questions:
		What Is The Core Focus Of The Protest
		What Are The Secondary Focuses/Targets
		What Is The General Risk Level
		What Local Organizations Can We Partner With/Delegate Tasks To
		How Often Should We Meet For Planning
		What Is The Investment Level Of The Organizers/Organizations Individually And As A Whole (Other Projects, Resources, Labor Availability)
		What Other Resources Do We Have At Our Disposal (Money, Spaces, Comrades, etc?)
		What Is The Optimal Location (Impact, Transportation, Safety)
		What Is The Optimal Timing (Day Of Week, Time Of Day, Semester/Yearly Cycles, Big Events, Weather)

	Flyer/Instagram Blurb:
		How far in advance should be posted and what time of day should it be posted
		What organizations can we collaborate with for the Instagram post
		Who/How Will We Print Our Flyers
		Who Can We Get To Pass Out Flyers
		Who/When/What Group Chats Will We Post The Flyer In
		Where Should We Flyer
		who can we get to make it, When Will We Have It Finished By
		
	Banner/Signs:

		What should be the language for the signs
		What old signs can we reuse/banners And Who Has Them
		Banner/construction (Sign Holders, Sign Supplies, Banner Supplies, Banner Transportation)
		who can we get to make them, When Will We Make Them/Have Them Finished By
		
	Press Release:
		When Will It Go Out
		Who Will Make It
		Who Is The Media Liaison
		What Information Do We Want To Share
		Do We Have Photos Of Similar Protests To Add To It
